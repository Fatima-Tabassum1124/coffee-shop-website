Our story

Started in a garage. Still runs like one.

The Copper Kettle opened in 2019 with one roaster, two tables, and a promise: every cup gets full attention, whether it's your first visit or your five-hundredth.

We're a small team — three baristas, one roaster, and a baker who shows up at 5am so the croissants are ready before you are. We keep the menu short so we can keep it right.

Come sit for ten minutes or three hours. The wifi password is on the chalkboard, and so is a lot of other stuff you might need to know.
